Clear Ice Method

Directional freezing from top-down. The insulated base slows freezing from sides/bottom, pushing air bubbles and impurities downward. Water freezes clear from the top of each cavity. Excess cloudy ice collects in the reservoir below the silicone tray.

Print & Material Notes

Silicone tray: 3D print the positive mold, then cast in food-grade silicone (Shore A 30-40).

Insulated base: Print in PETG or ABS (food-safe). 3mm wall with air gap or fill with foam insulation. The base acts as a cooler to control freeze direction.

Tolerances: Tray should nest into base with ~1mm clearance per side. Flange rests on base rim.
